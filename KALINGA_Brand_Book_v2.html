Prologue

Kalinga means the kind of care a Filipino mother gives without being asked, and a Filipino father gives without announcing.

This brand starts where it should — small. With two products. One Filipino name. And a promise we can actually keep.

We are not a billion-dollar conglomerate. We are not pretending to be one. We are a Filipino household care brand that begins with soap and detergent, made for real Filipino homes, sold in real Filipino stores, by real Filipino hands.

Two products · One philosophy · Built to grow honestly
01 What KALINGA Is

Two products. One philosophy.

Both products do the same thing — they wash. One washes the body. One washes the clothes. Both are acts of family care. Both belong under the same name.

06 Packaging

Calm on a noisy shelf.

Filipino retail shelves are visually exhausting. KALINGA's advantage is stillness — clean white space, one hero color band, a name you can read across the store.

01

SAME ARCHITECTURE

Every pack — soap or detergent — follows the same vertical order: KALINGA wordmark, color band, product name, tagalog tagline, weight & origin.

02

ONE COLOR EACH

Soap owns blue. Wash owns green. From three meters away, anyone in the store knows which one they're looking at.

03

HONEST CLAIMS

No "99.9% germ kill" unless we tested it. No "dermatologist approved" unless one approved it. The pack says what's real.
