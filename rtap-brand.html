01 — Positioning

Not a bank.
Not a wallet.
The rail underneath both.

RTAP is a third-party financial access infrastructure — deployed exclusively across the RBS store franchise, connecting every branch to digital finance through licensed payment partners.

What RTAP is not
A Bank

No deposits, no lending. RTAP doesn't hold a banking license — it connects RBS stores to those who do.

What RTAP is not
An E-Wallet

RTAP isn't another balance to top up. It's the access layer that links every wallet and biller together.

What RTAP is
Infrastructure

A franchise-wide connectivity network for cash-in, bills, e-load, and e-wallet rails — standardised across every RBS store, scalable as the network grows.

+ Cash-In + Bills Payment + E-Load + E-Wallet Connectivity + QR Payments + RBS Store Network + Franchise-Exclusive
The catch — as simple as 1 + 1

1. Our own credits can only be spent inside — never cashed out.
2. GCash runs on money the owner loads up front.

Part 1 · Closed-Loop Credits
Spend it inside. Never cash it out.

Any wallet credit we issue — like R-Eload — is a closed-loop credit. It exists only to push a transaction through: buy prepaid load, or pay a utility bill. It is not transferable e-money — you can't withdraw it, send it to a friend, or treat it as cash.

Top up cash → R-Eload credit → Load / Bills ✕ No cash-out  ·  ✕ No transfer

Think arcade tokens — useful inside the arcade, worthless outside it.

Part 2 · The GCash Catch
The owner pre-funds the wallet.

To offer GCash, the machine owner advances their own capital first — depositing money into Dafoxtech's corporate bank accounts (UnionBank, PNB, Security Bank). Dafoxtech then credits them with a digital eCash Wallet balance. Every GCash cash-in a customer makes is drawn down from that pre-loaded balance.

Owner deposits capital → Dafoxtech bank → eCash Wallet → Sell GCash

Think of stocking a store — pay for inventory up front, then sell from your stock.

07 — How It Works

Cash goes in.
Digital comes out.

A walk-up bridge from physical cash to the digital economy — powered entirely by licensed partners. No app, no account, and no money ever held by RTAP.

01

Go to your nearest RBS

RTAP lives inside the RBS store franchise. Walk into any branch — there's nothing to download and no account to register.

02

Find the RTAP kiosk

Look for the free-standing RTAP kiosk on the floor. Tap the screen to begin and choose what you're paying — cash-in, bills, or e-load.

03

Insert your cash & coins

Feed bills into the bill acceptor and coins into the coin acceptor. The kiosk counts your money in real time, down to the last peso.

Bill acceptor + coin acceptor
04

Confirm the destination

Enter the e-wallet number or biller and tap to confirm. RTAP hands the transaction straight to a licensed payment partner.

05

Take your receipt

Your payment lands in seconds and the kiosk prints your receipt. RTAP only moves the money — it never holds it.

✓ No app, no account ✓ Cash & coins accepted ✓ Settled by licensed partners ✓ RTAP never holds your money
