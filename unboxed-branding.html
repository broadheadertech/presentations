[ Chapter 01 ]
The Provocation
UNBOXED / 2026
Manila, Philippines

For 60 years, Filipinos were told that clean comes in a sachet.

Five pesos. One wash. One plastic packet.

Multiply by 110 million people, 365 days a year. The Philippines is now the third-largest ocean plastic polluter on Earth.

The industry called it "affordable accessibility."

We call it a box we never agreed to be put in.
