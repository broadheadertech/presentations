Park and charge on a network that actually works.

Spark is the software layer for a combined parking + EV-charging operator — the live-monitored network, the identity & billing engine, and the apps that make it effortless.

Live charger status One wallet · park + charge OCPP 1.6-J Exact-centavo billing
The problem

EV charging here is a leap of faith.

Dead-plug roulette

You drive to a charger with no idea if it's online, occupied, or broken.

Two systems, one car

Parking and charging are separate operators, payments, and queues.

Cash & surprise fees

Loose change, manual receipts, pricing nobody can see itemized.

Hardware, no software

Operators own chargers and gates — but nothing to run them.

The solution

One network. One tag. One wallet.

A combined park-and-charge network you can trust — every charger reports live — paid from one prepaid balance, billed exactly to the centavo.

Live-monitored

Every charger speaks OCPP in real time — drivers and staff see the whole network.

One account

UHF tag for parking, card for charging, plate on file — one wallet.

Prepaid load

Reload like expressway RFID. Spent across every site; never negative.

To the centavo

Metered live; settled against your wallet with an exact, itemized receipt.

How it works

From sign-up to receipt.

01

Register & get your tag

Sign up online with your plate; an attendant links your UHF tag + card.

02

Load your wallet

Reload online or over the counter — good across the whole network.

03

Tap in

Present your tag; authorized in a second, the barrier lifts or charger unlocks.

04

Charge or park, live

kWh and time metered in real time — watch it from the app.

05

Auto-billed on exit

Priced against the site tariff, settled from your wallet — to the centavo.

06

See everything

Balance, live session, and itemized history in your console.

Materials · charging

Any OCPP 1.6-J charger plugs in.

The one hard rule: the charger speaks OCPP 1.6-J and lets you set a server URL. Then it's brand-agnostic — buy on price and availability.
